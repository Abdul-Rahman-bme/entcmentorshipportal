Expectations from Mentors

Guidance and Time Commitment

Mentors should provide guidance on career pathways, offer feedback on student projects, assist with research or internship applications, and work with 2-5 students. They should meet with mentees at least once every two weeks, providing tailored support aligned with their expertise.
